# Governance Overview

## Purpose

Data governance provides the structure, accountability, and coordination required to support successful reporting operations.

The purpose of governance is to ensure that reporting processes are consistently managed, responsibilities are clearly defined, communication occurs across teams, and reporting activities can be sustained over time.

Effective governance helps organizations maintain reporting accuracy, reduce operational risk, and improve collaboration between business and technical teams.

## Governance Objectives

The governance framework supports:

* Clear ownership and accountability
* Consistent reporting processes
* Effective communication across teams
* Timely issue resolution
* Sustainable reporting operations
* Knowledge transfer and continuity
* Compliance with reporting requirements

## Governance Framework

Successful reporting requires coordination across multiple functional areas.

```
Business Owners
       ↓
Data Owners
       ↓
Technical Teams
       ↓
Validation Teams
       ↓
Reporting Approvers
```

## Annual Governance Review

Governance information should be reviewed at least annually.

Review:

* Ownership assignments
* Communication procedures
* Escalation procedures
* Supporting documentation
* Reporting responsibilities

Document any changes and communicate updates to stakeholders.
